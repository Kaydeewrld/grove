UNITED STATES DOLLAR (USD) $
  • Global Acceptance

    USD is one of the most widely accepted currencies worldwide. Supporting USD ensures that our users can easily participate in international markets and access a broader range of services and products.

  • Stability

    The USD is known for its stability compared to other currencies. By transacting in USD, Nigerian users can mitigate some of the risks associated with currency fluctuations and inflation in the local currency (NGN).

  • Convenience

    Many international transactions, including imports, exports, and online services, are priced in USD. Supporting USD simplifies these transactions, eliminating the need for multiple currency conversions and reducing associated costs.

BRITISH POUND (GBP) £
  • Global Relevance

  • The British Pound is one of the strongest and most stable currencies in the world. Supporting GBP allows our users to engage seamlessly with the UK market and beyond.
NIGERIAN NAIRA (NGN) ₦
  • Local Relevance

    The Nigerian Naira is the official currency of Nigeria. Supporting NGN allows our users to engage seamlessly with local businesses, services, and markets.

  • Convenience

    Transacting in NGN eliminates the need for currency conversion, making financial transactions straightforward and hassle-free.

KENYAN (KES) KSh
  • Cost-Effective

    By supporting KES, we help our users avoid the fees and charges associated with currency conversion, providing a more cost-effective financial solution.

  • Convenience

    Transacting in KES eliminates the need for currency conversion, making financial transactions straightforward and hassle-free for users operating within Kenya.
